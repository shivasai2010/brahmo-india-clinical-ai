# Clinical Data Sources - BRAHMO India Clinical AI Assistant

## Overview
This document lists the clinical data sources used in the BRAHMO India Clinical AI Assistant project.  
All data was manually researched to ensure relevance for Indian clinical practice.

---

## 1. Type 2 Diabetes Guidelines

### Source:
- Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) Guidelines
- American Diabetes Association (ADA Standards of Care 2024)

### Used For:
- First-line treatment recommendations
- HbA1c target guidance
- Metformin usage guidelines

---

## 2. Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)

### Source:
- KDIGO Clinical Practice Guidelines
- National Kidney Foundation (NKF)

### Used For:
- Metformin safety in renal impairment
- Drug dosage adjustments in CKD
- Contraindication rules

---

## 3. Heart Failure Management

### Source:
- European Society of Cardiology (ESC) Guidelines
- American Heart Association (AHA)

### Used For:
- Diabetes management in heart failure patients
- Medication safety considerations

---

## 4. Drug Information & Safety

### Source:
- India Drug Database references (CDSCO)
- Publicly available pharmaceutical references (Indian market)

### Used For:
- Drug safety checks
- General prescribing guidance
- Clinical alerts for contraindications

---

## 5. AI Clinical Reasoning Layer

### Source:
- Google Gemini AI (LLM reasoning engine)

### Used For:
- Clinical question interpretation
- Structured medical response generation
- Decision support synthesis

---

## Notes

- All clinical logic is designed for **decision support only**
- No patient-specific real data is stored
- System is intended for **educational + assistive clinical use**
